Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

TriangleCalc is a triangle solver application developed by WhoaStra Survey. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed the app to operate without collecting any personal data.

No Data Collection

TriangleCalc does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Usage or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or hardware information
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

Local Calculations

All triangle calculations are performed entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server or third party. The app functions fully offline.

No Analytics

TriangleCalc does not include any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or tracking SDKs. There are no first-party or third-party analytics of any kind.

No Internet Required

TriangleCalc does not require an internet connection to function. The app operates completely offline and makes no network requests.

Children's Privacy

Since TriangleCalc does not collect any data from any user, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Since the app collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our GitHub repository at github.com/whoastra/TriangleCalc.
